  2. Magcharging Inc., Shenzhen, P.R.C., Intern, 2019.7 - 2019.9.

    3. •  Ratio frequency wirless charging.

      We realize a prototype machine of ratio frequency wireless charging system. In transmitter-side, two USRPs are utilized to control three directional antennas for energy emission; in receiver-side, an intergrated device including an RF harvester and an RF-DC convertor is exploited for energy havesting. We also build a backscattering link for real-time optimization of transmitted signal based on blind adaptive beamforming algorithm. The receiver-side power is up to 12mW at the distance of 2m and 6mW at 3m. Specifically, it includes the techniques:
       - MIMO, blind adaptive beamforming.
       - Backscattering communication.
    Here is our real-time video: Triple-antenna 2m, Triple-antenna 3m.
  3. Optical Wireless Communication and Network Center in USTC, Anhui, P.R.C., Postgraduate Student, 2016.9 - 2019.6.

    4. •  Optical network system:

      The conventional media access control of wireless local area network (WLAN) can hardly support the transmission of big-data in the future, and the upcoming optical network tends to be accessed in a multipoint pattern for big-data transmission. We devise a doub-station access protocol based on CSMA/CA (DS-CSMA/CA) for optical wireless local area network (OWLAN), and propose the the algorithm to numerically maximize the throughput versus initial contention window and partner distribution map.

    Paper: G. Wang, C. Gong, Z. Jiang, and Z. Xu, "A Double-station Access Protocol for Optical Wireless Scattering Communication Networks." [ArXiv]

    •  Multiple access system:

      We study the physical-layer characteristics of multiple access for weak signal optical communication including multi-user and multi-service communications, where the scenairos of multi-user communication are single transmiter to multiple receivers and multiple transmitters to mulitple receivers; and the multi-service communication is to simutaneously transmit several types of serives with different rates. Furthermore, it includes the techneques:
       - Numerical optimization of multiple access achievable rates.
       - Multi-user estimation, signal processing, correcting code.
       - Hidden Markov Model.

    Papers:

    • G. Wang, C. Gong, and Z. Xu, "Signal Characterization for Multiple Access Non-line of Sight Scattering Communication." [PDF]
    • G. Wang, C. Gong, Z. Jiang, and Z. Xu, "Multi-layer Superimposed Transmission for Optical Wireless Scattering Communication." [PDF]

    •  Weak signal optical communication:

      We realize a real-time weak signal optical communication system based on FPGA over 1km transmission link with 1Mbps throughput (most advanced over the world at present), which contains the techniques:
       - Photon conunting technology.
       - Digital signal processing (synchronization, estimation & symbol detection).
       - Error correcting code (convolutional, RS, LDPC code).

    Paper: G. Wang, K. Wang, C. Gong, D. Zou, Z. Jiang, and Z. Xu, "A 1Mbps Real-time NLOS UV Scattering Communication System with Receiver Diversity over 1km." [PDF]
